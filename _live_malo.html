From Frame to Finish, Built the Malo Way.

Premier Commercial and Residential Steel Erection Company

We are pleased to introduce Madison Homes LLC dba Malo Construction Company as a professional, safety-focused, and dependable steel builder. Malo Construction Company specializes in metal building erection, pre-engineered metal buildings (PEMB), red iron and cold-formed steel systems, and both commercial and residential steel structure installations. Our experienced crews handle structural framing, metal roofing systems, wall and insulation panel installation, and turnkey steel erection services, always prioritizing safety, efficiency, and precision.

We are fully equipped to manage complete builds or subcontracted scopes, handling all crew logistics, accommodations, and mobility to ensure seamless project execution.

Our active service coverage includes: Texas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Alabama, Georgia, Florida, Ohio, Michigan, Indiana, Pennsylvania, Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Arkansas, Oklahoma, Missouri (St. Louis), New Hampshire, Maine, and surrounding regions.

Malo Construction Company is fully insured and maintains a reputation for professionalism, quality workmanship, and timely completion. We are confident that our capabilities and commitment align strongly with industry standards. We look forward to the opportunity to be added as an approved builder and to demonstrate our reliability and quality on future steel construction projects.
