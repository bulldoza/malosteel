About MaloSteel

Quality You Can
Trust

"Built on Trust, Quality Workmanship, and Long-Lasting Customer Relationships."

MaloSteel is a professional, safety-focused steel erection contractor with a proven record of delivering precision-built structures on time and within budget. We specialize in metal building erection, pre-engineered metal buildings, red iron and cold-formed steel systems — for commercial and residential clients alike.

Our fully mobilized crews manage all project logistics, travel, and accommodations, enabling seamless execution across the nation. Whether you need a complete turnkey build or a subcontracted scope, we bring the same dedication to every project.

What We Do

Our Steel Erection
Services

From pre-engineered metal buildings to complex structural steel — we deliver complete, code-compliant builds with unmatched precision.

Metal Building Erection

Expert assembly and erection of all types of metal and steel buildings, from small commercial units to large-scale industrial facilities.

Pre-Engineered Metal Buildings (PEMB)

Specialized installation of manufacturer-engineered steel systems, maximizing structural integrity and accelerating project timelines.

Red Iron & Cold-Formed Steel

Precision erection of primary red iron structural frames and cold-formed steel secondary framing systems for commercial and residential applications.

Metal Roofing Systems

Complete installation of standing seam, R-panel, and structural metal roofing systems engineered for longevity and weather performance.

Wall & Insulation Panels

Installation of metal wall panels and insulation systems for energy-efficient building envelopes that meet current code requirements.

Turnkey Steel Erection

End-to-end project management and erection services — we handle the complete steel scope so you can focus on the bigger picture.

Structural Framing

Heavy structural steel framing for commercial buildings, warehouses, distribution centers, and multi-story construction projects.

Residential Steel Construction

Custom steel framing solutions for residential applications — barndominiums, workshop buildings, agricultural structures, and custom homes.
